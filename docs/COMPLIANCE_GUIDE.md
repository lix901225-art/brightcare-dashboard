# BrightCare OS — BC Compliance Guide

## Overview

BrightCare OS is designed to help licensed BC childcare centres maintain compliance with the **Community Care and Assisted Living Act** and regional health authority requirements.

---

## Section 56(f) Daily Log

**Requirement**: All licensed facilities must maintain a daily log of events.

### What to Record
- Minor injuries not requiring medical attention
- Behaviour changes or concerns
- Illness symptoms observed
- Important parent communications
- Any other notable events

### How to Use
1. Go to **Records** > **Daily Log** tab
2. Click **Add entry**
3. Use child **initials only** (not full names) — BC privacy requirement
4. Select category and describe the event
5. Mark follow-up if needed

### Retention
- Export CSV monthly for archival
- BC requires retention for **minimum 1 year** after the child leaves the programme

## ECE Certification Requirements

**Requirement**: All staff working with children must have valid ECE certification.

### Tracking in BrightCare
1. Go to **Compliance** in the sidebar
2. View ECE certification status for all staff
3. **Red banner** = expired certifications (immediate action required)
4. **Amber banner** = expiring within 30 days (renew soon)

### Certification Levels
- ECE (Early Childhood Educator)
- ECE Assistant
- ECE Infant/Toddler
- ECE Special Needs
- Responsible Adult

## Incident Reporting

### When to Report
BC licensing requires formal incident reports for:
- Injuries requiring first aid or medical attention
- Any of the 20 Vancouver Coastal Health categories (aggressive behaviour, falls, medication errors, etc.)
- Events requiring parent notification
- Events requiring health authority notification

### Process
1. Go to **Records** > **Incidents** tab (or standalone Incidents page)
2. Create incident with severity level
3. Complete BC compliance fields
4. For formal VCH reporting, fill in the "Vancouver Coastal Health fields" section
5. Notify parent (recorded in system)
6. Complete follow-up actions
7. **Lock** the incident after resolution
8. Export **VCH PDF** for health authority submission

### Reporting Deadlines
- **Critical/Death**: Notify licensing officer **immediately** (within 1 hour)
- **Serious injury**: Within **24 hours**
- **Other reportable**: Within **5 business days**

## Child-to-Staff Ratios (BC Standards)

| Age Group | Ratio | Max Group Size |
|-----------|-------|---------------|
| Infant (0–18 months) | 1:4 | 12 |
| Toddler (18–36 months) | 1:4 | 12 |
| Preschool (30 months – 5 years) | 1:8 | 25 |
| School-age (5–12 years) | 1:10 | 24 |
| Multi-age | 1:8 | 8 |

Monitor ratios on the **Compliance** page dashboard.

## Immunization Records

**Requirement**: BC requires immunization records on file for all enrolled children.

### BC Core Vaccine Schedule
- DTaP-IPV-Hib (2mo, 4mo, 6mo, 18mo)
- Pneumococcal conjugate (2mo, 4mo, 12mo)
- Rotavirus (2mo, 4mo)
- MMR (12mo, 4-6yr)
- Varicella (12mo, 4-6yr)
- Meningococcal C (12mo)

Track completion per child on:
- Each child's profile page (Immunization section)
- Compliance page (Immunization records card)

## Health Authority Inspection Preparation

### Checklist
- [ ] All ECE certifications current and on file
- [ ] Daily log up to date (Section 56f)
- [ ] Incident reports complete with follow-ups closed
- [ ] Child-to-staff ratios in compliance
- [ ] Immunization records complete for all children
- [ ] Emergency contacts documented for all children
- [ ] Care plans current (asthma, allergy, etc.)
- [ ] Medication administration records accurate
- [ ] Fire drill records up to date
- [ ] Facility licence displayed

### In BrightCare
- **Compliance** page shows overall readiness score
- Each child's profile shows **Enrolment readiness** checklist
- **Records** page centralises all required documentation
