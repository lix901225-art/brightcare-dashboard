# BrightCare OS — Parent Guide

## Getting Started

1. You'll receive an invite link from your centre
2. Click the link and sign in with your email via Auth0
3. Your account is automatically linked to your child's centre

## Viewing Attendance

1. Tap **Attendance** in the menu
2. See today's check-in/check-out times for each child
3. Use the weekly view to see the past week
4. The **This month** card shows your attendance rate percentage

## Viewing Daily Reports

1. Tap **Daily Reports** in the menu
2. Reports show meals, naps, mood, activities, bathroom, and notes
3. Tap a report to see the full detail with photos
4. Mood is shown with emoji (😊 Happy, 😌 Content, 😐 Neutral, etc.)

## Viewing Bills and Subsidies

1. Tap **Billing** in the menu
2. The **Fee summary** card shows a clear breakdown:
   - Total childcare fees
   - Less: CCFRI (Child Care Fee Reduction Initiative)
   - Less: ACCB (Affordable Child Care Benefit)
   - **Parent portion** = what you actually owe
3. Tap an invoice to see line items and payments
4. The **Year-end tax summary** shows your total for CRA Line 21400

### Downloading Tax Receipts

1. In the Billing page, scroll to "Year-end tax summary"
2. Click **Download** to get your receipt
3. Report the eligible amount on your CRA tax return under **Line 21400 — Child Care Expenses**
4. Keep receipts for at least 6 years

## Reviewing Incidents

1. Tap **Incidents** in the menu
2. See all incident reports for your children
3. Tap **Review** to acknowledge you've read the report
4. Your review timestamp is recorded

## Messages

1. Tap **Messages** in the menu
2. View conversations with your centre's staff
3. Type a message and press Enter (or tap Send) to reply
4. Messages are delivered in real-time — no need to refresh

## Switching Language

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Under Language, select your preferred language:
   - English
   - 简体中文 (Simplified Chinese)
   - 繁體中文 (Traditional Chinese)
   - ਪੰਜਾਬੀ (Punjabi)
   - Tagalog
3. The interface will switch immediately

## Need Help?

Contact your centre's staff through the **Messages** feature.
